Your current rate is 3.67% gross / 3.75% AER (variable). Interest is calculated daily on your full balance and paid on the last day of each statement quarter. Interest is paid gross, without tax deducted; whether you owe tax on it depends on your personal circumstances and allowances.

## What AER means

AER (Annual Equivalent Rate) shows what the interest rate would be if interest were paid and compounded once each year, so you can compare savings accounts fairly. The gross rate is the flat rate before compounding within the year is taken into account.
