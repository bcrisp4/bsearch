## DISABILITY INCOME DETAILS

**Total Disability means that, solely because of Injury or Sickness, You are unable to perform the material and substantial**

duties of Your Occupation, and You are not working in any other occupation. We will treat You as Totally Disabled if You have a loss of the use of two limbs, or total loss of sight, speech or hearing, even if You are able to work.

**Residual Disability means that, solely because of Injury or Sickness, You are working in Your Occupation or another**

occupation but Your Income is reduced by at least 20% of Prior Income. A Residual Disability benefit is payable after the Elimination Period in proportion to the percentage of Prior Income lost; where the loss exceeds 75%, We pay the full Monthly Benefit Amount.

**Your Occupation means the occupation, or occupations if more than one, in which You are regularly engaged at the time**

Disability begins. If You are working in a professional or technical specialty, Your Occupation means that specialty.

**Successive Periods of Disability resulting from the same or a related cause are treated as one continuous period unless**

separated by Your return to full-time work for at least 6 consecutive months.

**Waiver of Premium. After You have been Disabled for 90 consecutive days, We will waive premiums that become due**

while Disability continues, and refund premiums paid for that 90-day period. Premium payments resume on the first premium due date after Disability ends.

**Rehabilitation Benefit. If You participate in a vocational rehabilitation program approved by Us in writing, We will pay the**

reasonable costs of the program not covered by other insurance or public funding, in addition to any other benefit payable.

**Presumptive Recovery. Benefits end on the earliest of the date You are no longer Disabled, the end of the Maximum**

Period of Payment, and the date You fail to provide required proof of continuing Disability.

## EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS

1. **We will not pay benefits for a Disability caused by, contributed to by, or resulting from war or an act of war, declared or**

undeclared, or Your active participation in a riot.

2. **We will not pay benefits for a Disability caused by an intentionally self-inflicted injury.**
3. **We will not pay benefits for any period of Disability during which You are incarcerated.**
4. **We will not pay benefits for a Disability caused by Your commission of, or attempt to commit, a felony, or Your**

engagement in an illegal occupation.

5. **We will not pay benefits for a Disability resulting from cosmetic surgery, except that reconstructive surgery following a**

covered Sickness or Injury, or surgery to donate an organ, is treated as a Sickness.

6. **Benefits for a Disability due to Mental Disorder or Substance Abuse are limited to a combined lifetime maximum of 24**

months of benefit payments, except while You are confined in a hospital or institution licensed to treat the condition.

7. **The Pre-Existing Condition Limitation on the policy face page applies to conditions, whether diagnosed or**

undiagnosed, for which You received advice or treatment, or experienced symptoms that would cause an ordinarily prudent person to seek advice or treatment, within 24 months before the Coverage Effective Date.

8. **Benefits are not payable for any period of Disability during which You are not under the Regular Care of a Physician**

appropriate for the disabling condition, unless We agree in writing that continued care would be of no benefit.

## OTHER FEATURES

**Transplant and Cosmetic Surgery. If You are Disabled because You donate an organ, or because of complications of**

pregnancy, the Disability is treated as a Sickness under this policy.

**Concurrent Disability. A Disability caused by more than one Injury or Sickness, or by both, is treated as one Disability;**

benefits are not doubled.

**Suspension During Unemployment. If You are unemployed for at least 60 consecutive days, You may suspend this policy**

for up to 12 months. No premiums are due and no benefits are payable during suspension; coverage resumes without evidence of insurability when premium payments restart.
