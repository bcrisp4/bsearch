# Statement of Principal Terms of Employment

Issued in accordance with the employment-rights legislation. Employer: Halewick Systems Ltd ("the Company"). Employee: Theo

Senior Platform Engineer, Platform Engineering 4 August 2025. No employment with a previous employer counts towards continuous employment. £37,554 (thirty-seven thousand five hundred and fifty-four pounds) per annum, payable monthly in arrears on or around the 25th of each month by credit transfer, subject to deductions for tax and National Insurance. Salaries are reviewed annually in April; a review does not guarantee an increase. 37.5 hours per week, Monday to Friday. The Company operates hybrid working: you are expected to attend a minimum of two days each week at the London office. Occasional additional hours may be needed to meet the needs of the business; no overtime is payable, but time off in lieu may be agreed for sustained out-of-hours work, including scheduled on-call participation which attracts the allowance set out in the Platform Engineering on-call policy. 6 Farrier Court, London EC2Y 9QX, and your home address when working remotely. You will not be required to work outside the UK for more than one month at a time. 25 days per holiday year (January to December) plus public holidays, accruing pro rata in your first year. Up to five unused days may be carried into the first quarter of the following year. Statutory sick pay is payable in accordance with legislation. In addition, after probation the Company pays full salary for up to four weeks of certified sickness absence in any rolling twelve months, inclusive of statutory entitlement. You will be enrolled in the Company's workplace pension scheme. The Company contributes 5% of qualifying earnings, and the standard employee contribution is 4%, which you may vary within scheme rules. The first three months are a probationary period, during which either party may end the employment on one week's written notice. The Company may extend probation once, by up to three months, where performance or attendance needs further assessment. After probation, the notice required from either party is one month's written notice, rising by one week for each complete year of service after four years, up to the statutory maximum. The Company may pay in lieu of notice, and may place you on garden leave for all or part of any notice period. You must not, during or after your employment, use or disclose any confidential information of the Company or its customers except in the proper performance of your duties or as required by law. Nothing in this clause prevents a protected disclosure under whistleblowing legislation. Inventions, software, designs and other works created by you in the course of your duties belong to the Company to the fullest extent permitted by law, and you agree to do what is reasonably necessary (at the Company's expense) to perfect its title. Personal open-source contributions made outside your duties and without Company confidential information are unaffected; the Engineering open-source policy explains the approvals needed for contributions connected with your work. None apply to this employment.
