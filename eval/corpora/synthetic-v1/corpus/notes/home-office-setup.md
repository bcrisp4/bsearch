# Home office (second bedroom)

WFH 2-3 days a week for Halewick so this needed doing properly.

The room's single socket was on the far wall, so everything ran off a
daisy-chained extension lead for the first two months — bad. Got
Brayfield Electrical in on 18 Nov: new switched double socket on a spur
on the desk wall, plus they swapped the hallway pendant for the fitting
I'd bought. £227.52 incl VAT (3 hrs labour + materials), minor works cert
included. Landlord approved it in writing beforehand, receipt filed in
case it comes up at deposit time.

Desk faces the window, monitor arm clamped to the back edge. The mini PC
lives under the desk now too — see the homelab inventory note.
