# Condensation, winter 25/26

First proper cold snap (Dec) = water pooling on the bedroom window sill
every morning, black speckles starting in the corner by February. Flat is
a 60s block, so no surprise.

What actually helped, in order:

1. Squeegee the window each morning (30 seconds, unreasonably effective)
2. Lid on pans, bathroom door SHUT + extractor running 20 min after shower
3. Moved the bed 10cm off the exterior wall
4. Cheap hygrometer — turns out bedroom was hitting 72% RH overnight.
   Now 58-62%
5. Trickle vents open even when cold. Yes it feels wrong

Didn't buy a dehumidifier in the end. Wiped the speckles with the mould
spray, hasn't come back since the routine changed.

The whistling window seal on the snagging list probably counts as
"ventilation" tbh.
