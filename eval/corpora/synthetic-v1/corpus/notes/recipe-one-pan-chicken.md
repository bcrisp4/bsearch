# One-pan chicken thighs, the tuesday one

The recipe I make so often it deserves version control.

Serves 2 with lunch leftovers.

- 6 chicken thighs, bone in skin on (always thighs, breast is a scam)
- 1 lemon, 1 whole garlic bulb halved sideways
- new potatoes, halved, more than seems right
- green olives, big handful
- oregano, chilli flakes, too much olive oil

Oven 200C. Potatoes + oil + salt in the pan 20 min head start (this is
the whole trick, v1 skipped it, v1 potatoes were bullets). Everything
else on top, skin up, 40 min more. Rest 5. Pan juices on bread,
mandatory.

v3 changes: preserved lemon instead of fresh when Rosh brings a jar.
Feta crumbled on after, if it's a bad-week tuesday.
