# Lisbon — 4 nights, 21-25 May 2026

With Rosh. Flights booked (out Thu early, back Mon evening). Apartment in
Alfama, third floor, "some stairs" they said. 87 stairs, I counted.

## Plan vs what happened

| Day | Plan | Actually |
|---|---|---|
| Thu | land, wander Alfama, early dinner | ✓ plus got beautifully lost, best part |
| Fri | Belém morning, MAAT afternoon | Belém queue insane, skipped the monastery interior, pastéis were the point anyway (6 of them. six.) |
| Sat | day trip Sintra | ✓ Pena palace at 9am beats the crowds, Quinta da Regaleira the actual highlight |
| Sun | flea market, miradouros, fado | market ✓, fado replaced by 3-hour dinner, no regrets |
| Mon | Time Out Market, fly home | ✓ overrated but convenient, calibrating expectations helped |

## Notes for next time

- Tram 28 is a queue with a tram attached. Walk or bus
- The prepaid transport card thing (viva viagem) — load cash on it, works
  on everything incl the Sintra train
- May weather: perfect. 24°, breeze. This is the month
- Apartment aircon didn't exist, fan did. Fine in May, would not be in Aug
- Budget: came in ~£640 my share incl flights, the travel sinking fund
  covered all of it. Smug.

Would return: yes, for the Gulbenkian (missed) and more Sintra.
