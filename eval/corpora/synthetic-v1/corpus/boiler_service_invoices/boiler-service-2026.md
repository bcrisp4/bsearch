### Safety advisory - please read

During the service the engineer noted that the flue terminal sits at the minimum permitted clearance from the adjacent openable window. This is not immediately dangerous and no action is required now, but if the window frame is ever replaced or extended the clearance must be re-measured, and we recommend keeping the window closed while the boiler is running for long periods. The appliance is approaching five years in service. The heat exchanger shows early scale deposits typical for the local water hardness; fitting an in-line scale reducer at the next service (approx. £60 fitted) would extend its life. A carbon monoxide alarm was present and tested - replace its battery when the alarm's low-battery chirp begins, and replace the alarm itself by the expiry date printed on its case.
