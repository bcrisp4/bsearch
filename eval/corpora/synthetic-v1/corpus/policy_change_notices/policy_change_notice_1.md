# Important information. Changes to your home contents insurance policy.

We review the wording of our home contents insurance policies each year. This notice describes the changes that will apply to your policy from your renewal date on or after 1 July 2021. Please read it alongside your policy booklet and your schedule, and keep it with your policy documents. If anything here is unclear, or if a change affects how you want to be covered, call us on 0113 496 0330.

## What's changing

- We have increased the excess for escape of water claims from £250 to £450. This reflects the rising cost of tracing and repairing leaks; all other excesses on your schedule are unchanged.
- We have added a requirement to maintain your home's water system: where a claim arises from a leak at a pipe joint that shows long-standing corrosion, we may reduce or decline the claim.
- We have clarified the definition of "storm" to mean wind speeds of at least 55mph, hail severe enough to damage hard surfaces, or extraordinary rainfall or snowfall.
- We have removed cover for theft of contents from outbuildings unless the outbuilding was locked and shows signs of forced entry.
- We have updated the "matching sets" wording: where an item forming part of a matching set is damaged, we pay only for the damaged item, not for undamaged items that no longer match.
- Our claims line has a new number, 0113 496 0344, available 24 hours a day. The old number will continue to redirect for twelve months.

Everything else about your policy stays the same. Your renewal invitation, showing your premium and any changes to your personal details or sums insured, will reach you separately around three weeks before your renewal date. If you do not wish to renew, tell us at any time before your renewal date and your cover will simply end - there is no charge for not renewing.
