# Important information. Changes to your home contents insurance policy.

We review the wording of our home contents insurance policies each year. This notice describes the changes that will apply to your policy from your renewal date on or after 1 July 2023. Please read it alongside your policy booklet and your schedule, and keep it with your policy documents. If anything here is unclear, or if a change affects how you want to be covered, call us on 0113 496 0330.

## What's changing

- Why premiums are rising this year: the cost of replacing furniture, electronics and building materials has risen sharply, and so has the average cost of claims. Your renewal invitation will show your own premium; this note explains the market-wide pressure behind it.
- Please check you are not underinsured. Your contents sum insured should reflect the full cost of replacing everything in your home as new. If your sum insured is too low, any claim payment may be reduced in proportion - for example, cover for two-thirds of the true value pays twothirds of a claim.
- We have introduced index-linking: your contents sum insured will now increase automatically each month in line with a published replacement-cost index, so inflation does

not erode your cover between renewals. You pay nothing extra until renewal.

- Following a flood or escape of water claim, you can now choose resilient repairs - such as water-resistant materials - where they cost no more than a like-for-like reinstatement.
- We have strengthened our fraud wording: if any part of a claim is deliberately exaggerated or supported by false documents, the whole claim is forfeit and the policy may be cancelled from the date of the fraud.
- We have simplified our complaints process to a single point of contact, with a promise of acknowledgement within two working days. Details are in the updated policy booklet.

Everything else about your policy stays the same. Your renewal invitation, showing your premium and any changes to your personal details or sums insured, will reach you separately around three weeks before your renewal date. If you do not wish to renew, tell us at any time before your renewal date and your cover will simply end - there is no charge for not renewing.
