## What a gift like yours makes possible

£150.00 funds a full day of specialist nurse cover on our heart helpline - a lifeline for people newly diagnosed with a heart condition, and for their families, at the moment they need clear answers most.

Gifts like yours also power our research programme. This year we are funding fourteen research fellowships into inherited heart conditions, which often go undetected until they affect a young, apparently healthy person. Early findings from work we funded are already changing how families are screened after a diagnosis. Closer to home, our community defibrillator fund placed 212 public defibrillators last year, each registered with the ambulance service and maintained by trained local volunteers. Survival after cardiac arrest more than doubles when a defibrillator is used in the first few minutes. We will keep you posted on the difference your support makes through our supporter magazine, Heartbeat.
