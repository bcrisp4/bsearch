# From 1 September 2025, the annual interest rate on your loan changes from 4.3% to 4.6%.

Interest rates on Plan 2 loans are linked to inflation and are reviewed every September using the inflation figure from the previous March. Because you are in repayment, the rate you are charged also varies with your income, up to a maximum of inflation plus 3%; the new rate above is the rate that applies at your current income level.

**This change does not alter your monthly repayments. What you repay each month depends only on your income -**

9% of anything you earn above the repayment threshold - and is collected through your employer's payroll as usual. The interest rate affects how your outstanding balance grows, not what you pay. Interest is calculated daily on your outstanding balance and added monthly. You can check your balance, see the rates applied to your account, and make voluntary repayments at sfagency.example/account. Yours sincerely,
