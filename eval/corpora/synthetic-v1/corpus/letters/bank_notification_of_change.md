# We're making changes to your Personal Banking Terms

Dear Mr Marchbank, We review our Personal Banking Terms and Conditions and our charges regularly. This letter tells you about changes that affect your Everyday Current Account (sort code 12-98-76, account number ending 8274).

## These changes take effect on 18 May 2026

You don't need to do anything if you're happy with them.

## What's changing

- Arranged overdraft interest is going down. The interest rate on arranged overdrafts falls from 34.9% EAR (variable) to 33.9% EAR (variable). If you use an arranged overdraft, borrowing will cost slightly less.
- Same-day CHAPS payments will cost less. The fee for sending a same-day CHAPS payment from a branch falls from £25 to £15. CHAPS payments made through the app remain £12.
- Free text alerts are switching on for everyone. From the effective date we will automatically send you a text alert when your balance falls below £50 or an unpaid transaction fee is about to apply. You can change or turn off alerts in the app at any time.
- New payees will need app approval. When you set up a payment to someone new using telephone banking, you will be asked to approve it in the Ferrand Mobile app before it is sent. This protects you if someone impersonates you on the phone.
- Cheques will clear faster. Cheques paid in at a branch or by post will be available by 11.59pm on the next working day, in line with cheques paid in through the app.

## If you're not happy with these changes

You can close your account, or switch to another provider, at any time before 18 May 2026 without charge and without notice. If you keep using your account after that date, the updated terms will apply. The full updated Personal Banking Terms and Conditions are available now at ferrandbank.example/terms, in any branch, or by post on request.
