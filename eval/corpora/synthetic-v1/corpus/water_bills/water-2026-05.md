### Why this bill shows two sets of prices

Water charges are set for each charging year running from 1 April. This bill spans the change, so usage up to 31 March is charged at 2025-26 prices and usage from 1 April at 2026-27 prices. We split your metered usage between the two periods in proportion to the days in each. From 1 April 2026 the water rate is £2.19 per m³ (was £2.07) and the wastewater rate £1.40 per m³ (was £1.31); daily standing charges rise by 0.6p and 0.8p. For a typical flat that's about £14 a year more.
