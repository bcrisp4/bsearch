### The January price cap, explained

Every three months the industry regulator sets a cap on the unit rate and standing charge a supplier can charge on a variable tariff. The cap is not a limit on your total bill - use more, pay more. From 1 January the cap level rose, and Fair Deal Flex prices rose with it (we wrote to you last month). Your new prices remain below the cap level. This bill covers December, so it still uses your old prices; your new prices appear from next month's bill.
