# Discounts, exemptions and support

The full charge assumes at least two adults live in the property. If you live alone the bill is reduced by 25%. Students, some carers and some other groups aren't counted. If your income is low you may qualify for council tax reduction of up to 100% - apply at netherhale.example/ctr.

**You must tell us within 21 days of any change that affects your bill - for example someone moving in or out, or losing**

entitlement to a discount. Failing to do so can lead to a penalty.

**If you don't pay. If an instalment is missed we send a reminder; if payment**

isn't brought up to date you lose the right to pay by instalments and the full balance for the year becomes due, and we may seek a liability order with costs added.

### Struggling to pay? Contact us before missing a payment - we can

reschedule instalments over twelve months and check whether you qualify for council tax reduction.

**Appeals. If you think you're not liable, or a discount has been wrongly refused,**

write to us first. If you disagree with our decision you can appeal to the independent valuation tribunal. Making an appeal does not let you withhold payment.

**Where the money goes. A summary of council spending and each precepting**

authority's share is at netherhale.example/counciltax/spending.
