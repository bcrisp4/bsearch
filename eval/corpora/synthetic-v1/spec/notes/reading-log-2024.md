# Reading log 2024 (the Brooklyn famine)

Six books. SIX. The subway should have been reading time but the subway
was podcast time and the podcasts were mostly about other people
reading.

Worth recording:

- **The Harbor Variations** — NYC novel read IN NYC, everyone said do
  it, they were right
- **Latency** — techno-thriller, dumb, devoured in a weekend at the
  Rockaways in April (too cold, great chips)
- **Notes on a Small Apartment** — essays; funny because true; 412
  Calver Street featured in spirit throughout
- Three others not worth the shelf space they're currently shipping
  across the Atlantic anyway ("ship less", exhibit A)

2025 resolution made standing in a bookshop in Fort Greene: commute =
book time, wherever I end up. (2026 update: it worked.)
