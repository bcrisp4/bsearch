# Furniture for the flat — shortlist and what actually happened

Aug 2025. Budget was "don't be silly", i.e. no number, i.e. danger.

Sofa candidates:

- Calder & Roe "Askern" 2.5-seater — moss green velvet, oak legs, £749.
  The one I kept coming back to.
- The grey corner sofa from the shop on the high street — bigger, £1,100,
  would've dominated the room
- Secondhand? Looked, but everything decent needed a van and two friends
  I don't have yet on this side of the Atlantic

Went with the Askern, obviously. Added the Weld side table (£129) to the
same order, £45 for two-person delivery to the room = **£923 all in**.
Ordered 23 Aug, delivered 14 Sep. The three weeks of sitting on a camping
chair were character building.

Mattress was a separate order (the online place with the 100-night trial
thing). Kept it — verdict: fine, not life-changing, marketing is a hell
of a drug.

Still want: proper bookshelf, reading lamp, a rug that isn't the doormat
promoted beyond its ability.
