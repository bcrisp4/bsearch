# Standing charge dispute — Harwood Energy

Noticed on the September bill that the standing charge line worked out at
51.2p/day when the Fair Deal Flex tariff says 48.9p. Went back through and
they'd been doing it since June (before my first full bill even).

- Raised it via chat, **14 Sep 2025**. Agent initially insisted 51.2p was
  "the capped rate" which is not how any of this works.
- Escalated, got a callback two days later, they admitted the error.
- October bill: £2.12 credit, line item "Standing charge correction
  (Jun–Aug)", plus a printed apology panel actually referencing my
  complaint date. Credit where due, the paper trail was good.

Total win: £2.12. Time spent: ~90 minutes. Principle: priceless etc.

Lesson: check the pence-per-day maths on the first bill of any new tariff.
