# Banana bread, no thinking version

3 dead bananas, 100g melted butter, 100g sugar (was 150, nobody
noticed), 1 egg, 190g SR flour, pinch salt, cinnamon.

Mash, mix wet, fold dry, loaf tin, 160C fan ~50min, skewer test.

Dark chocolate chunks > chips. Walnuts if they exist. It's not a
recipe it's a banana disposal protocol.
