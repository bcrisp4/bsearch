LISTS OF ACCEPTABLE DOCUMENTS
All documents containing an expiration date must be unexpired.

LIST A
Documents that Establish Both Identity and Employment Authorization

  1. U.S. Passport or U.S. Passport Card
  2. Permanent Resident Card or Alien Registration Receipt Card
  3. Foreign passport that contains a temporary I-551 stamp or notation
  4. Employment Authorization Document that contains a photograph
  5. For an individual temporarily authorized to work for a specific employer, foreign passport with an Arrival/Departure Record (Form I-94) bearing the same name and an endorsement of the individual's status
  6. Passport from certain freely associated states with Form I-94

LIST B
Documents that Establish Identity

  1. Driver's license or ID card issued by a State, provided it contains a photograph or identifying information
  2. ID card issued by federal, state or local government agencies
  3. School ID card with a photograph
  4. Voter's registration card
  5. U.S. Military card or draft record
  6. Military dependent's ID card
  7. Native American tribal document
  8. Driver's license issued by a Canadian government authority

For persons under age 18 who are unable to present a document listed above: school record, clinic record, or day-care record.

LIST C
Documents that Establish Employment Authorization

  1. A Social Security Account Number card, unless the card includes a restrictive endorsement
  2. Certification of report of birth abroad issued by the consular authorities
  3. Original or certified copy of a birth certificate issued by a State, county, or municipal authority bearing an official seal
  4. Native American tribal document
  5. U.S. Citizen ID Card
  6. Identification Card for Use of Resident Citizen
  7. Employment authorization document issued by the federal immigration authority (not a List A document)
Acceptable Receipts. A receipt showing that the individual has applied to replace a document that was lost, stolen or damaged may be presented in place of the document, and is valid for 90 days from the date of hire.
