Interest is paid gross, without tax deducted; whether you owe tax on it depends on your personal circumstances and allowances.

What AER means

AER (Annual Equivalent Rate) shows what the interest rate would be if interest were paid and compounded once each year, so you can compare savings accounts fairly. The gross rate is the flat rate before compounding within the year is taken into account.
