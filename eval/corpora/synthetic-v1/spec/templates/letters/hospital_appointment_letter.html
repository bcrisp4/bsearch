Please arrive ten minutes early and check in at the East Wing Outpatients reception desk on Level 2.

What to bring with you

  • This letter, and your appointment card if you have one.
  • A list of all medicines you currently take, including anything you buy over the counter, with doses if possible.
  • Any relevant letters or test results you hold, including correspondence from clinicians who treated you abroad.
  • Glasses or hearing aids, if you use them.
  • A note of any questions you want to ask — appointments go quickly and it is easy to forget.

Getting here and parking

Pay-and-display patient parking at the Lakeview Road entrance is limited and fills early; please allow extra time or use public transport if you can. Local buses stop directly outside the main entrance on Lakeview Road, and the nearest rail station is around a fifteen-minute walk. Accessible parking bays for blue badge holders are beside the East Wing entrance. If a medical condition means you cannot travel by private or public transport, you may qualify for non-emergency patient transport: call 020 7946 0518 at least two working days before your appointment.

If you cannot attend

If this appointment is not convenient, or you no longer need it, please call the appointments line on 020 7946 0512 as soon as possible, quoting your hospital number, so we can offer the slot to another patient. If you do not attend and have not told us, we may return your care to your GP.

If you need an interpreter, a signer, or this letter in large print, braille or audio, call the appointments line and we will arrange it free of charge.
