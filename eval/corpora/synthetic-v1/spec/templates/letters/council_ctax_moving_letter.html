How to pay

The easiest way to pay is by Direct Debit — set it up in two minutes at netherhale-borough.example/counciltax or by calling 020 7946 0400. You can also pay online by card, by standing order, or at any payment-network outlet using the barcode on your bill. Whichever way you pay, please quote account number {{ vendor.council_tax_ref }}.

You must tell us if your circumstances change

The single person discount only applies while you are the sole adult resident. If anyone aged 18 or over moves in, or you move out, you must tell us within 21 days. Failing to report a change that affects your discount can lead to a penalty.

Think the band is wrong? You can ask the national valuation body to review your property’s band. You must carry on paying while any appeal is considered.

Struggling to pay? Contact us early — we can spread payments over 12 months, and you may qualify for council tax support depending on your income.
