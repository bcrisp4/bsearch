Caring for your velvet upholstery

Brush weekly with a soft clothes brush following the pile, keep out of strong direct sunlight, and blot spills immediately — never rub. Seat cushion pressure marks are normal for velvet and relax with plumping. Professional cleaning only; do not machine wash covers.
