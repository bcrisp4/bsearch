About the ratings

The energy efficiency rating is a measure of the overall efficiency of a home on a scale of 1 to 100: the higher the number, the lower the running costs. The environmental impact rating is a measure of the home’s impact on the environment in terms of carbon dioxide emissions — again, the higher the rating, the lower the impact. The average rating for a dwelling in England and Wales is band D.

What can I do today?
  • Check that your heating system thermostat is not set too high and that radiators in unused rooms are turned down.
  • Wash laundry at lower temperatures and only run full loads.
  • Turn off lights and appliances at the wall rather than leaving them on standby.
  • Close curtains at dusk to keep heat in, and check that they do not drape over radiators.
