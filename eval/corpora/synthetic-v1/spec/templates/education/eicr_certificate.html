Guidance for recipients of this report

This report is an important and valuable document which should be retained for future reference. It describes the condition of the fixed electrical installation only; it does not cover portable appliances. The “Overall assessment” box in Section 5 states whether the installation is satisfactory or unsatisfactory for continued use. Where the assessment is unsatisfactory, items classified C1 or C2 should be remedied as a matter of urgency by a competent person, and FI items investigated without delay. C3 items are recommendations for improvement: the installation is safe for continued use, but the improvement would enhance protection. Landlords are reminded of their statutory duty to ensure that electrical safety standards are met throughout a tenancy, to provide a copy of this report to each tenant before occupation, and to any prospective tenant on request, and to carry out remedial work within the statutory period where required.
