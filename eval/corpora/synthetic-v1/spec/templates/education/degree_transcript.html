Grading scheme

Units are marked on a percentage scale with a pass mark of 40. Honours classifications are awarded on the credit-weighted average of Levels 5 and 6: First-Class Honours 70% and above; Upper Second-Class Honours 60–69%; Lower Second-Class Honours 50–59%; Third-Class Honours 40–49%. One credit corresponds to ten notional hours of learning; 120 credits are studied at each level. Two ECTS credits correspond to one unit credit.
